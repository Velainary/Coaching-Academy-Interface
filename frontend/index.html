About EduConnect

EduConnect is a modern learning management solution for coaching academies. We help institutions manage their students, instructors, courses, and attendance with ease. Our goal is to make education management simple, transparent, and effective for everyone.

  • 📘 Streamlined course organization
  • 👩‍🏫 Instructor profiles and teaching insights
  • 🕓 Real-time attendance tracking
  • 💬 Parent-student-teacher communication made easy
